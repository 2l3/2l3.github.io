Welcome Editor is a service that let you change/add a default message when someone get in your DM

Twitter was offering it, but it got shut down


When and how to show the Welcome Message in you DM ?

  • When someone open your DM for the first time
  • If you change the message, those who already saw it will see the new one 7 days after that
  • When your conversation is deleted and they come back to DM you
These rules are decided by Twitter and cannot be changed
